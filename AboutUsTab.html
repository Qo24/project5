About us
Our greatest glory is not in never failing, but in every time we fall.

The International University of Sarajevo is a private university located in the capital of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Sarajevo. The University was founded by the Foundation for the Development of Education in the period 2004-2005. IUS is open to students from all over the world and the language of instruction and communication is English. It offers a four-year education according to the Bologna system. IUS has 1,650 students from 55 countries and faculty members from 20 countries who perform academic and research activities in a variety of science disciplines, such as:Engineering,Art,Social sciences,Psychology,Political sciences,Architecture

Our story

Even though this is the first offical season of our basketball club, the idea of forming it was there for a much longer period of time than most of the people know. Even though this was finalized in 2021 the project regarding the club began in 2019 when two second year students came up with an idea to form a basketball club where only students of the University would be able to play. The idea and the plan was going great and our first squad was prepared to play an exibition game which was intended to be played at campus in order to get more eyes on the club. However, a day before the game was supposed to be played COVID-19 was introduced to the world and with it the first lockdown of Bosnia and Herzegovina. This resulted in clubs activities being pushed back along with the idea of playing the 2020 season. However, even that couldn't have stopped our two students Mirnes Mesanovic and Ahmed Plasto to persever with their idea and after the long 2 years their idea came into existence.
